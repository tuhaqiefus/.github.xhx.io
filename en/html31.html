Quzhou, China
Construction Content
Cleanroom wall panels and ceiling panels, air showers, automatic doors, clean booth installation, and ductwork.
Project Period

The HVAC and cleanroom ancillary equipment engineering for the Hyosung Film VC3 Project is a critical industrial construction project located in Quzhou, China. With a contract amount of 3.4 million RMB, this project highlights its professionalism and value in the field of specialized industrial environment construction. It represents a significant undertaking in precision manufacturing and high-standard environmental control.

The construction content of this project is highly specialized, primarily focusing on providing high-standard cleanliness and air quality control for Hyosung Film's production environment. Specific tasks include the installation of cleanroom wall panels and ceiling panels, which form the physical barrier of the cleanroom; the installation of air showers and automatic doors to control contamination when personnel and materials enter and exit clean areas; and the installation of clean booths and ductwork, which are core components for maintaining internal air circulation and cleanliness within the cleanroom.

The entire engineering project spanned from May 9, 2019, to July 31, 2021. During this period, the project team focused on the precise installation and commissioning of various cleanroom and HVAC-related equipment to meet the stringent environmental requirements of the Hyosung Film VC3 Project. The timely completion of the project is crucial for ensuring the normal operation of the client's production line and product quality control.

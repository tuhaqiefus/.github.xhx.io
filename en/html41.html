ISO 14001

    The company has been ISO 14001 certified for 10 consecutive years.

Production Environment Management

    In daily production operations, Xinhuaxia strictly adheres to the requirements of the ISO 14001 environmental management system. This means that environmental protection is integrated into every stage, from raw material procurement to product delivery. We are committed not only to reducing energy consumption and waste generation during production but also to actively taking measures to prevent pollution and continuously optimizing environmental performance. Through the strict guidance of ISO 14001, we ensure that all production activities comply with the highest environmental standards, providing customers with green, sustainable products and services.

Waste Classification

    Beyond our production processes, we extend the requirements of the ISO 14001 environmental management system to our daily office operations. We actively promote office waste classification by setting up sorted recycling bins in office areas, guiding employees to correctly dispose of recyclable materials like paper, plastic, metal, and glass, as well as other waste. Through meticulous classification management, we effectively reduce the amount of office waste generated, increase resource recovery rates, and collectively foster a greener, more environmentally friendly office environment.

Volunteer Environmental Sanitation

    We encourage our employees to actively participate in environmental protection initiatives. We regularly organize environmental volunteer activities, such as participating in community clean-ups, tree planting, and environmental awareness campaigns. These activities not only enhance employees' environmental awareness and teamwork spirit but also demonstrate the company's determination to give back to society and build a better home for all.

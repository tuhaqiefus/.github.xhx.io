Social Responsibility

& Fairness

We actively undertake social responsibility and ensure fairness and equal opportunities in employment.

Employment and Fair Practices

We provide equal employment opportunities regardless of race, gender, religion, nationality, or age. Our company is composed of citizens from 5 countries, with women making up approximately 30% of our workforce.

Occupational Health

& Safety

By implementing the internationally recognized ISO 45001 standard, we prioritize the health, safety, and well-being of our employees.

ISO 45001 Certification

We strictly adhere to the ISO 45001 Occupational Health and Safety Management System standard, investing significant resources in safety training to enhance employees' safety awareness and emergency response capabilities; we also improve our accident reporting and investigation mechanisms to ensure a safe and healthy workplace for all employees.

Community Engagement

& Philanthropy

We firmly believe in the importance of giving back to society and actively participate in and support various charitable activities, contributing to community development.

Community Contributions

As corporate citizens, we actively engage in philanthropy and community building. We support public welfare projects in areas such as education, environmental protection, poverty alleviation, and aid to the disadvantaged through various forms, including donations, material assistance, and employee volunteer services.
